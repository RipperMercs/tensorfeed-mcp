# Code of Conduct

This project values clear communication, technical rigor, and good faith.

## What we expect

- Treat others with basic respect. Critique ideas, not people.
- Stay focused on the technical question at hand.
- Assume good intent. Most disagreements are misunderstandings.
- Disclose conflicts of interest when relevant (employer, financial stake, competing product).

## What is not welcome

- Personal attacks, slurs, or targeted intimidation.
- Off-topic political or ideological campaigning in issues or PRs.
- Spam, vendor pitches disguised as feature requests, or AI-generated noise PRs.
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may close issues or PRs, lock threads, or block accounts that violate this code. Decisions are final and not subject to appeal in the same thread. Pattern-of-behavior bans are documented privately.

## Reporting

Report concerns to `evan@tensorfeed.ai`. Include the URL, what happened, and what outcome you would like to see. Reports are read by the maintainer and not redistributed.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, PRs, discussions, the npm package, the MCP registry listing, and any future associated forums or chat channels.
